Components

Modal

Modals are used as pop ups to show more detail for Listings, to allow for editing of anything, and generally whenever the data requires a "drill-down" approach. Modals will not respond to keyboard "Esc" keys, but should be tab-navigatable. Each modal will have a "Close" button in the top right corner, and at least one "Close" or "Cancel" button in the bottom left. In addition, other "action" buttons will be in the modal footer. Every modal must have a title, and that title must be labeld with an aria-labeled-by id.
